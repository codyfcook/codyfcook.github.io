Cody Cook

Welcome! I am a PhD student in Economics at Stanford GSB, where I focus Urban and Public economics research.

I previously worked with John List on a research team at Uber. My research at Uber included projects looking into the gender pay gap, retirement and the gig economy, how drivers learn, and heterogenous responses to incentives. I grew up in rural New Hampshire and did my undergraduate degree at the Unversity of Chicago.

If you have any questions or comments about my research—or just want to chat—I'd love to hear from you. My email is codycook@stanford.edu
