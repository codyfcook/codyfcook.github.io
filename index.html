Biography

Welcome! I am a 2nd-year PhD student in Economics at Stanford GSB, focusing on public economics and industrial organization. Some of my research interests include renewable energy, housing, and funding scientific research.

I grew up in Evanston, IL, but have been at Stanford nearly continuously since 2016. During the year between undergrad and graduate school I worked for Prof. Matthew Gentzkow and hiked the Pacific Crest Trail.

If you have any questions or thoughts about my research, my email is zkashner@stanford.edu.
