I am an assistant professor in the Department of Economics at Yale. My fields of study are urban, public, and industrial organization. In my research, I study topics including affordable housing, congestion pricing, and urban inequality.

I went to Stanford GSB for my Ph.D., UChicago for undergraduate, and grew up in rural New Hampshire.
