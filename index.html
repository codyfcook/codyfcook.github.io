Cody Cook

I am a PhD student in Economics at Stanford GSB. My fields of study are labor, public, and industrial organization. Currently, my research has two focuses: 1) online labor markets, such as Uber, and 2) the US housing market. CV available here.

Before beginning my PhD, I worked for John List on a research team at Uber. My research at Uber focused on driver side of the platform, with projects analyzing the gender pay gap, retirement and the gig economy, how drivers learn, and heterogenous responses to incentives. I completed my B.A. at the University of Chicago, where I worked with Brent Neiman on the effects of sales taxes. I grew up in New Hampshire and still love the outdoors -- skiing, hiking, anything in the mountains.
