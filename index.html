About

I am a public economist with research interests in urban and labor economics. In my research, I use large linked datasets and tools from industrial organization to study topics such as affordable housing, congestion pricing, and urban inequality.

I can be reached at codycook@stanford.edu.

I am on the job market in the 2023-2024 academic year.
