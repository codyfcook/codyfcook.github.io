About

I am a public economist with research interests in urban and labor economics. In my research, I use large-scale datasets and tools from industrial organization to study topics such as affordable housing, congestion pricing, and urban inequality.

I will be joining the Department of Economics at Yale after spending one year as a postdoctoral fellow at the Cowles Foundation.

I grew up in rural New Hampshire, then went to the University of Chicago for my undergraduate degree and Stanford GSB for my Ph.D.

You can reach me at codyfcook@gmail.com or cody.cook@yale.edu
